Beta Characters get a copy of your account, which makes this week's beta event a good opportunity to financially ruin your account for science. So I went ahead and gambled. Like a good scientist, I'm writing my results down right here.

I will submit these results to the official wiki, and interested communities ([fast] and OTC). Please don't re-submit them there.

I made screenshots before/after every batch, and on every bonus BLC drop. Due to disk space constraints, I was unable to record video.

175 Black Lion Chests / Keys

Starting out with none of the exclusive drops unlocked. The common drop shown ingame as "2 Black Lion Statuettes" appears to yield 2-4 Statuettes. Every chest drops one guaranteed Statuette and one seasonal item (currently Evon's Trick or Treat bag), which I didn't bother to write down.

tl;dr: 12 bonus drops out of 175 keys, that's 1 in ~14.3. But the "exclusives" appear to be more common than other "uncommon" items..
